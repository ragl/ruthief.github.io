About Ruth

Born and raised in Virginia, I received my Bachelors degree in Political Science (minor in Sociology) followed by my Masters in Teaching from Christopher Newport University before setting out on my own into the great unknown.

I fell in love with the technology world via working at two technology non-profits, the Technology Association of Oregon (formerly SAO) and the Drupal Association. My background and personal skills have been a wonderful fit in the client/account support area. I excel at assessing efficiency gaps, possess a broad strategic perspective, and am a problem solver legislating on behalf of client needs. My natural people skills with a teaching degree aids me in being an information liaison between the tech team and the client. It all really boils down to a great respect and appreciation for the coding geniuses of the world and a commitment to excellent customer service and business needs.

In my free time I enjoy a local latte preferably in giant bowl size with a good book or marathoning my Netflix account. Once over caffeinated, I like getting out and enjoying Oregon's amazing natural beauty via volunteering, skiing, hiking, and in warmer months hope to return to rowing. I have a baking habit, forcing others to enjoy piles of delicious cookies, pies, etc. Currently I am still mid quest to perfect my family's southern cooking recipes into gluten-free form.
